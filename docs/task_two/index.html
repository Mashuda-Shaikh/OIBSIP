Biography

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg was born on May 14, 1984, in White Plains, New York. From a young age, he displayed a strong interest in computers and software development. While studying at Harvard University, he launched a site called "The Facebook" with his roommates in 2004.

The platform quickly grew beyond college campuses and evolved into one of the most powerful social networking platforms globally. Zuckerberg left Harvard to focus on Facebook full-time, leading it to success with billions of users worldwide.
